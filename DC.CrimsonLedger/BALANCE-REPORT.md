## How to read this

- **Power** = mean HP-equivalent swing vs the 10-card baseline deck over an 8-turn scripted combat, averaged across 3 enemy profiles x 3 seeds (delta vs the same deck without the card — energy opportunity cost is priced in automatically).
- **ON-CURVE** means inside the shipped pool's P5-P95 for that rarity; the Band column shows where. P25-P75 is the comfortable middle.
- **INSUFFICIENT-SIM** is not a balance judgement. It means the card's behavior is mostly outside the simulable DSL subset; it needs gate-4 in-game QA.
- **DEGENERATE-RISK** flags unbounded replay/copy/conjure loops or damage still accelerating at the 16-turn cap. Verify by hand.
